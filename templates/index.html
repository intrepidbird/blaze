Blaze - A Fire Idea.

Blaze is designed to make people of all races, ethnicities, genders, and more feel comfortable in a safe, caring online community. From advocating for diversity and creativity, to allowing users to create multicultural pages with factual informaation, kindness and welcoming are indispensable in Blaze. We want to aid different communities, and help them vocalize their information, accomplishments, and more, by creating Wiki's, that can be utilized for numerous purposes such as advocacy groups, research findings, portfolios and more. We believe in excellence, and excellence is found through diversity.
